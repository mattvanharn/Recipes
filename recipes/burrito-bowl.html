Burrito Bowl

Description

This burrito bowl contains chicken, rice, and whatever toppings you want to include. It is easy and delicious!

Ingredients

For the chicken

  • Chicken breast
  • Chipotle peppers in adobo
  • Lime juice
  • Garlic
  • Avocado oil
  • Salt

For the cilantro lime rice

  • White rice
  • Cilantro
  • Lime juice & zest
  • Salt

Toppings

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Avocado
  • Black beans
  • Corn
  • Red onions
  • Cilantro

Instructions

Marinate and cook the chipotle chicken

  1. Season the chicken and allow it to marinate for a couple hours.
  2. Then cook the chicken on a skillet until heated through

Cook the cilantro lime rice

  1. In another pot, cook white long grain rice and then adding to it fresh lime juice and cilantro.
  2. Stir the rice with the lime juice and cilantro and well combined an set aside.
